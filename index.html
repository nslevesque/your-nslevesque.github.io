Future of Privacy Forum
Launching the Center for Artificial Intelligence
The Challenge

AI's rapid growth created urgent need for trusted, nonpartisan policy guidance. FPF needed to establish its Center for AI as a credible global leader in ethical AI governance — differentiated from similar initiatives.

The Approach

Developed the Center's brand and "trusted convener" messaging, secured media coverage, engaged 220+ corporate members, aligned the launch with the inaugural AI Forward Summit, and built a full content ecosystem.

Results
  • Trade & government media coverage for the Center for AI and AI Forward Summit
  • High attendance from policymakers, industry leaders, and researchers
  • Endorsements from privacy leaders reinforcing credibility
  • Consistent on-message communications across all FPF staff
Fight Colorectal Cancer
Revitalizing Brand & Digital Presence
The Challenge

Fight CRC's visual brand was confused with a competing organization, and its outdated website lacked the patient resources and advocacy tools its community needed most.

The Approach

Led a full brand overhaul and website redesign featuring a Patient Resource Center and Advocacy Action Center, driven by advocate buy-in and launched via community-led social media campaign. Also redesigned Beyond Blue Magazine, earning two national design awards.

Results
  • Email subscribers up 25%; website traffic up 26%
  • Advocates became brand ambassadors, amplifying reach organically
  • Action Center drove measurable policy participation
  • Won 2020 Aster Gold & GDUSA Health & Wellness Awards
Future of Privacy Forum
Rebuilding a High-Performing Comms Department
The Challenge

FPF's communications program needed a complete rebuild — with new staff, systems, and a PR agency — to match the organization's growing influence in global tech policy.

The Approach

Hired and mentored a team, onboarded a PR agency, implemented a project brief process, and developed a crisis communications framework for rapid response.

Results
  • 90% increase in media mentions in under three years
  • 47% boost in social media engagement
  • 20% growth in email opt-ins
  • Nearly 130 placements in 2023 including NYT, Axios, Politico, The Hill
Maryland Coalition Against Sexual Assault
"Rape Is Not Your Fault" Campaign
The Challenge

Increase sexual assault reporting and advance prevention education across Maryland's college campuses and communities in partnership with the Maryland Department of Health and Mental Hygiene.

The Approach

Developed and executed the "Rape Is Not Your Fault" campaign in collaboration with the Baltimore mayor's office, Baltimore City Police, and the local SART office. Recruited a graduate social impact graphic designer from the Maryland Institute and College of Art to lead creative.

Results
  • 29% increase in sexual assault reporting within one year
  • Positive local media sentiment amplified campaign reach and credibility
  • Enhanced cross-collaboration between city partners and community stakeholders
